‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Are generic medicines as effective as branded ones?
Yes. Generic medicines contain the same active ingredient in the same dose. They must pass bioequivalence tests to prove they work identically. The WHO and CDSCO both certify generics as equally effective.
How do I find a Jan Aushadhi store near me?
Visit janaushadhi.gov.in/store-locator or search "Jan Aushadhi Kendra near me" on Google Maps. There are 10,000+ stores across India. Many are located near government hospitals.
Can I ask my doctor for a generic prescription?
Yes, absolutely. You have the right to ask for generic prescriptions. Under CDSCO guidelines, doctors should prescribe by generic name. Many doctors are happy to accommodate this request when patients ask politely.
