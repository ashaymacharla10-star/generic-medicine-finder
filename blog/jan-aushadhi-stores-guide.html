What is Jan Aushadhi?

The Pradhan Mantri Bharatiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) is a Government of India initiative launched in 2008 and significantly expanded since 2015. The Bureau of Pharma PSUs of India (BPPI) runs this scheme to provide quality generic medicines at affordable prices to all citizens.

As of 2025, there are over 10,000 Jan Aushadhi Kendras (stores) in all states and union territories across India, including in rural and semi-urban areas.

Are Jan Aushadhi Medicines Safe and Effective?

Yes. Jan Aushadhi medicines must meet the same regulatory standards as branded medicines:

  • All manufacturers must be WHO-GMP certified
  • All medicines are CDSCO approved
  • Third-party quality testing is mandatory
  • Samples are regularly tested by BPPI at NABL-accredited labs
