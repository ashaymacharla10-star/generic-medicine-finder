Antibiotic Resistance Warning

While generic antibiotics are safe and effective, India has a serious antibiotic resistance problem. Misuse and overuse of antibiotics ‚Äî whether branded or generic ‚Äî creates resistant bacteria that won't respond to any antibiotic.

  • Never take antibiotics without a prescription
  • Always complete the full prescribed course, even if you feel better
  • Don't share antibiotics with others
  • Don't save leftover antibiotics for future use
  • Don't take antibiotics for viral infections (cold, flu) ‚Äî they don't work on viruses
