‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is generic Atorvastatin as good as Lipitor or Atorva?
Yes. Atorvastatin is one of the most studied medicines in the world. Every approved generic version must prove bioequivalence to the original. Your cholesterol control will be identical with a generic.
Should I take statins morning or night?
Most statins (especially shorter-acting ones like Simvastatin and Lovastatin) are better taken at night since cholesterol production peaks at night. Longer-acting statins like Atorvastatin and Rosuvastatin can be taken at any time. Follow your doctor's instructions.
Do generic statins cause more muscle pain (myalgia)?
No. Muscle pain (myalgia) is a known side effect of statins in general ‚Äî not of generic versions specifically. The same dose of the same molecule causes the same side effect profile regardless of brand. If you experience muscle pain, consult your doctor.
