The Key Difference: Dosage

The only meaningful difference between Dolo 650 and Crocin 500 is the dose. Dolo 650 gives you 650mg per tablet, while Crocin gives 500mg. Both are Paracetamol.

A higher dose (650mg) is more effective for high fever and severe pain. For mild headaches or low-grade fever, 500mg is sufficient. There's no difference in quality or safety between the two brands ‚Äî it's purely a dose difference.
