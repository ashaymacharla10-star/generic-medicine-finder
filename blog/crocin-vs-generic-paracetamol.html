Is Generic Paracetamol Safe?

Yes, absolutely. All generic medicines sold in India must be approved by the Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation (CDSCO) ‚Äî the Indian equivalent of the US FDA. They must prove the same safety and efficacy as the branded version.

Generic Paracetamol has been used safely by hundreds of millions of people worldwide. Doctors at government hospitals routinely prescribe it. The WHO lists Paracetamol as an essential medicine.

Why is Generic Paracetamol Cheaper?

The price difference has nothing to do with quality. It comes down to costs:

  • Branded companies spend crores on advertising, celebrity endorsements, and doctor visits
  • Generic manufacturers don't spend on R&D (the molecule is off-patent)
  • Generic companies have lower packaging and marketing costs

You're paying for the brand name, not better medicine.

When to Choose Crocin vs Generic?

For most people, generic Paracetamol works identically to Crocin. However, in some cases you might prefer branded:

  • For children ‚Äî Crocin has specific pediatric formulations with exact dosing guides
  • When you need a specific combination (e.g., Crocin Cold & Flu)
  • Personal preference for a trusted brand you've used for years

Always consult your doctor if you're unsure.
